	\textbf{Research highlights} \\

	\begin{itemize}
		\item A novel non-intrusive RB method coupling proper orthogonal decomposition with multi-layer perceptrons. 
		\item Numerical studies for the nonlinear Poisson equation and the incompressible steady Navier-Stokes equations.
		\item Physical and geometrical parametrizations considered.
		\item Good agreement with the finite element method.
		\item Substantial speed-up enabled at the online stage as compared to a traditional RB strategy. 
		\item Sensitivity analysis on the number of training samples and neurons.
		\item Comparison between MLP-based regression and cubic spline interpolation within the RB framework. 
	\end{itemize}
